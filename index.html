About Me
Designing Is My Passion

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Engineering and have specialized in Computer Science. During my academic journey, I discovered a deep interest in the creative side of technology — which led me to pursue UI/UX design and graphic design. I’ve successfully completed internships as a UI/UX Designer at Exposys Data Labs, where I worked on intuitive app designs and user-friendly interfaces. I also served as a Web Development Intern at Prodigy InfoTech, gaining hands-on experience with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Currently, I work with Airmid Software Solutions as a Graphic Designer, creating marketing materials like posters, banners, and content for digital platforms. I’m passionate about blending creativity with technology to produce visually compelling and user-focused designs.

Skills Reflects Our Knowledge

I work with a wide range of design and editing tools that help bring creative ideas to life — from static visuals to dynamic video content. Using tools like CapCut, InShot, VN Editor, Premiere Pro, Filmora, Kinemaster, and Canva, I craft clean, modern, and purpose-driven designs. Whether I’m designing promotional materials or editing content for social media, I focus on quality, clarity, and creativity across every project.
